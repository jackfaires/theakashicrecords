ABSUNTI

Absunti, also known as Jeremiah Cornelius, is a Spoken Word Hip Hop artist from Baton Rouge, Louisiana. With a versatile style & incomparable creative ability, Cornelius aims to ignite an uncontrollable fire his own way. He was 17 when he realized he wanted to be purposeful in his creative ability. 2014 was the year his interest in lyricism began to evolve by getting involved with the non-profit organization Forward Arts. Having a desire to reflect passion and truth within his artistry, Absunti is intentional about giving his art a message with a focus on social awareness. With mental health being a passion of his, he encourages others of the culture to give their message to the world. Absunti's ultimate desire is to share his story while gaining knowledge that can potentially increase his overall life experience, as well as be a catalyst for necessary change. Cornelius is currently pursuing his undergraduate studies in Mass Communication and Broadcast Journalism at The Southern University and A&M College located in Baton Rouge, La. Aside from his creative collaborations with other independent artist, he has also been recognized by names such as the Baton Rouge Poetry Alliance and Red Stick Radio. Inspired by the many intricacies of life, Cornelius has big plans to advance the world as we all know it.
